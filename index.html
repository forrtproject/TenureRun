CONTROLS:

[LEFT/RIGHT] or Swipe to switch lanes
[UP/SPACE] or Swipe Up to jump

THE ACADEMIC TREADMILL

Welcome to Tenure Run! This game represents the struggle of early-career researchers navigating the modern academic publishing landscape.

  • 🧱 Paywalls: Traditional journals lock knowledge behind subscriptions. Hitting one ends your run!
  • 💸 APCs (Article Processing Charges): "Gold" Open Access journals charge massive fees (often $3k+) to publish. Jump over these to save your grant money!
  • 🟦 Open Science Badges: Collect these (representing open data/materials) to earn citations!
  • 💎 Diamond OA: Completely free to read AND publish. Grants you a shield!
  • 🔓 Open Access/Preprints: The Orange Lock. Uploading to a preprint server blasts you above the peer-review delays!
  • 😡 Reviewer 2: If you stumble, the dreaded, overly-critical Reviewer 2 will catch you!

REJECTED!

Reviewer 2 caught you, or you hit a Paywall.
